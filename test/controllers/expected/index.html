About

Strafforts is a web app that calls Strava API to fetch athlete's estimated best efforts and races data, then visualize them in forms of data tables, line charts and pie charts, in such way athletes can perform some analysis, like half marathon PB progression, fastest shoes used for a 10K race, how often 5K PB gets set, etc.

This app is absolutely free and open source. It doesn't modify any of your data on Strava or store your Strava password. All you need to do is to connect this app with your Strava account by clicking "Connect with Strava" button and wait for our background worker to fetch and visualize your data.
